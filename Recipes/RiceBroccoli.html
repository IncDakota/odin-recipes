Ingredients List

    1/2 Cup of Butter
    4 Tbsp of Minced Onion
    1 Bag of Frozen Broccoli Florets
    2 bags of boil-in-the-bag rice
    1 Can of Evaporated Milk
    1 Can of Cream of Chicken
    2/3 Jar of Cheez Whiz
    Seasonings to Taste

How to Make

    Melt the butter in a large soup pot over medium low heat
    Once melted, add the minced onion
    Add in the broccoli florrets and fold into the butter until tender
    Start boiling water in a medium saucepan while the broccoli florrets tenderize
    Once the water is boiling, add the rice in for the intended time
    When the broccoli is tender enough, cut it into small pieces with the same utensil you mixed it with. If you're not able to it need to tenderize for long
    Preheat oven for 350
    Add the cream of chicken, evaporated milk, and cheez whiz to the broccoli
    Fold the broccoli mixture and add spices
    When the rice is done, add it to the rice mixture
    Pour mixture into a bake-safe container
    Put aluminum foil over the pan
    Bake for 30 minutes before removing the aluminum foil and baking for another 30 minutes
    Enjoy!
